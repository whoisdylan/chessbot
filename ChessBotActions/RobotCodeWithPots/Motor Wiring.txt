Motor Wiring Setup

								   .--> Motor 1 --> Relay 1 --.
					 			   |--> Motor 2 --> Relay 2 --|
Speed Controller V+ 1--> V+ Rail --|--> Motor 3 --> Relay 3 --|--> V- Rail --> Speed Controller V- 1
								   '--> Motor 4 --> Relay 4 --'
 

Relay Pins

2 --> Relay 1 --> GND
3 --> Relay 2 --> GND
4 --> Relay 3 --> GND
5 --> Relay 4 --> GND

Speed Controller Wiring

Arduino pins 9 - 13 <--> Shield pins 9 - 13
Arduino GND <--> Shield GND <--> External GND
Shield Vcc <--> External Vcc
